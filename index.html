Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about tutoring

Is this suitable for absolute beginners?
Yes, absolutely! No prior programming knowledge is required. We start from the very basics like installing Python and writing your first line of code.
Do you offer one-on-one tutoring?
Yes, I offer personalized online sessions tailored to your specific learning goals and pace.
What age group do you teach?
I primarily teach high school students and university beginners, but I am open to anyone willing to learn!
What tools do I need?
You need a computer (PC/Mac) and access to the internet. We will set up VS Code and Python together in the first lesson.
